The Amazing Spider-Man (film)


The Amazing Spider-Man is a 2012 American
superhero film based on the Marvel
Comics character Spider-Man and
sharing the title of the character's
longest-running comic book series
of the same name.
It is the fourth theatrical Spider-Man film
produced by Columbia Pictures
and Marvel Entertainment,
The Amazing Spider-Man films.
